Welcome to The Hive

The Hive is a Social Media Web Application offered to Student Organizations found at a College or University. The Hive allows members of such Student Organization an innovative approach towards finding their new friends on Campus. The Student Organizations that are most likely to use The Hive are those that have mentor/mentee, big/little, sister/brother etc. programs. Not only can users discover their new friends, but admins have the ability to pair applicants with each other based on the user's profile and application on The Hive.
