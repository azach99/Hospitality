About

The Hive is a Social Media Web Application offered to Student Organizations found at a College or University. The Hive allows members of such Student Organization an innovative approach towards finding their new friends on Campus. The Student Organizations that are most likely to use The Hive are those that have mentor/mentee, big/little, sister/brother etc. programs. Not only can users discover their new friends, but admins have the ability to pair applicants with each other based on the user's profile and application on The Hive.

All users have the ability to update/view their profile, update/view their application, search for bigs and littles, and view alerts from the admins of the Student Organization. Admins have the ability to view both the profile (public to users) and application (private to specific user) for each applicant, search applicants based on a series of criteria such as first name, gender, user kind (big or little), and pairing key, and send alerts to all users. The most important ability for admins is the vital responsibility of pairing applicants with their closest friends for life. View some of the feautures of this Web Application below!
